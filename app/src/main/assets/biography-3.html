1951 He passed Matriculation Examination and got admitted to the City College to study commerce. His maternal uncle, a businessman and also his guardian, promised him a job of an accountant. In the same year, he became a member of the Communist Party of India .

1953 He passed Intermediate Commerce Examination, but gave up studying commerce and was admitted to the Presidency College , Kolkata to study Bengali literature. But he did not appear for the examination.
